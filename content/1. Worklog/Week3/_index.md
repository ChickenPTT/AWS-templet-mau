### Week 3 Objectives:

- Master knowledge about Amazon Relational Database Service (Amazon RDS) and database management features

- Practice Backup and Restore operations on RDS

- Master deploying applications on Lightsail (WordPress, PrestaShop, Akaunting)
- Understand Lightsail management features like Snapshots, instance scaling, and monitoring
